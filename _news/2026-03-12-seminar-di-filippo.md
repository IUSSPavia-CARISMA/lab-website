**Monitoring forest dynamics under climate change: Integrating multiple approaches and timescales**
Alfredo Di Filippo, Università della Tuscia, Viterbo

Forests are key ecosystems for climate change mitigation, sequestering and storing large amounts of carbon across the ecosystem. Their contribution to the carbon cycle varies along environmental gradients and may be significantly altered by climate change. This seminar presents an ensemble of approaches — spanning remote sensing and field survey — to monitor forest response to climate change across short and long timescales, with a focus on the role of natural forests in mitigation strategy.
